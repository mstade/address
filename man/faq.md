# Ran into trouble? Confused?

Don't worry, we've got you covered. The FAQ should answer the most common questions with address, and should you find what you're looking for isn't answered here then check out the [issues].

[issues]: https://github.com/zambezi-websdk/address/issues

Try searching the issues first, it's quite possible that someone else ran into a problem similar to yours, and found a solution. If that's the case, please do comment on the issue anyway, to let us know you were helped by it. This helps us determine whether problems are common, so we can deal with them appropriately!

If you can't find an answer to your question among the [issues], then feel free to [create a new one][new-issue]!

[new-issue]: https://github.com/zambezi-websdk/address/issues/new
